# Active Lanes

> Example content (Acme Studio). Replace with your own. "Lanes" are the parallel
> workstreams you run. The agent sweeps them on a cadence so nothing gets dropped.

**Cadence:** Morning brief → Afternoon check-in → End-of-day wrap.
**Principle:** Some days you're in one lane, some days all of them — but never lose track of any.

## Lane 3 — Pipeline / growth
Outreach to new leads, follow-ups, proposals out. 3 proposals pending.

## Lane 4 — Internal / product
The stuff that makes the business run better: automations, templates, the ops board.

### Sweep checklist (run each at its time)
- [ ] Morning: calendar, top-3 for the day, anything overdue in FOLLOWUPS.
- [ ] Afternoon: progress vs the top-3, unblock anything stuck.
- [ ] EOD: what shipped, update HANDOFF, tee up tomorrow's first move.
